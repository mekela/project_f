GOVERMANCE

PHILOSOPHY

Although not legally required to abide by a Corporate Governance Code, we have always been committed to the highest standards of corporate governance. We are proud to have implemented the guidance issued in the Corporate Governance Code for Unlisted Companies developed by the Institute of Directors (IoD) in the United Kingdom and the European Confederation of Directors’ Associations (ecoDA) in Europe. In addition, we also look to implement best practice principles from the full Corporate Governance Code issued by the Financial Reporting Council in the UK and other similar guidance when deemed appropriate by the Board of Directors given the size and scope of our organisation.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

The Board of Directors represents the Group's shareholders and is collectively responsible for the
long-term management and success of the company. The detailed role and responsibilities of the
Board are documented within its Board Charter.
