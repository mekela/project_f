ABOUT US

PROFILE

The Filuet Group was established in 1992 providing contract logistics and BPO solutions to foreign retailers entering the Russian market. Pursuing rapid organic growth within the Russian territory, Filuet expanded its footprint to the surrounding CIS countries. Today, the Group provides a full suite of international expansion solutions, mainly to large e-commerce, retail and direct selling clients. We are committed to enabling our clients access and thrive in foreign markets. By investing in talent and technological innovation we further develop our offering and help our clients’ realize their competitive advantage.

OUR VISION

To create a globally recognised brand that is synonymous with trust, technology and cross border trade.

THE MISSION

We help multinational corporations expand and operate in challenging markets using our market entry and development models.

Through the establishment and management of state-of-the-art fulfilment centers, complimented by a suite of innovative technologies and professional services, we enable our clients to access their chosen markets and amplify their international and urban product distribution capabilities.
