Introduction

Back in 2010, Herbalife approached us with a challenge to develop an effective and efficient product access solution that can operate 24/7 if needed. As usual, our reply was “no problem” and we proceeded to complile a team of experts in UI/UX software, payment systems and self-service technologies. In May 2011, the first prototypes of the Automated Sales Center (ASC) and Auto attendant (AA) were born. The project has since grown from strength to strength as evidenced below:
