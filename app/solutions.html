SOLUTIONS

MARKET OPENING

Onshore Online

Look to establish local presence & gain foothold
Develop your presence using our local infrastructure. Our VENI model, (which means 'come' in Latin) enables you to develop a market or region without necessarily having a legal presence, by using our fiscally approved financial models, infrastructure and a dedicated team, combined with our Business Process Outsourcing and Contract Logistics Services. VENI is truly a total outsourcing solution. The model encompasses within itself transportation services, brand and local product compliance, added value warehousing, payment collection and forwarding, sales and marketing support, customer service for before, during and after purchase. This solution offers free product circulation across the Eurasian Economic Union (Russia, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Armenia and Kyrgyzstan) and reaches out to the countries of the CIS
